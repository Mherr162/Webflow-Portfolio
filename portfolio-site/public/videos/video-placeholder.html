Video Placeholder

This is a placeholder for your video that will appear in the About section of your portfolio.

How to Replace This with Your Video:

  1. Prepare an MP4 video file (ideally 15-30 seconds, optimized for web)
  2. Name your video file (e.g., about-video.mp4)
  3. Place it in the public/videos/ directory
  4. Update the src attribute in AboutSection.tsx to point to your video:

    <InfiniteLoopVideo src="/videos/about-video.mp4" className="w-full aspect-video rounded-lg shadow-lg" />

Your video will automatically play in a continuous loop, without sound, providing a seamless visual experience in your portfolio.
